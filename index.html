My Journey

My passion for technology began not in a lecture hall, but with a simple frustration: the best digital tools were often inaccessible. This sparked my journey into programming. It's a testament to how awesome modern technology is that I could teach myself entirely through AI tutors and free online resources. I started with foundational courses in Python and essential business skills in Excel, all via Coursera, quickly realizing I could build the solutions I wanted to see.


This led me to a challenge: could I create a suite of tools that were not only powerful but also free, private, and intuitive? This question became my mission. I dove into web development, learned to design user-friendly interfaces with Canva for mockups and visuals, and applied my coding skills to build GeniusKit and EduGenius from the ground up—toolkits designed to empower students with smart, AI-driven utilities. My journey is one of continuous learning and a drive to build technology that helps others think and create more effectively.
